For this project I was part of the concepting team, the Interaction Designer, and front-end developer.

www.ecu.edu.au/degrees/overview

HBF Amaze

HBF Health Insurance knows that everyone has the potential to be healthy and amazing, and they want to help every person in WA to achieve that goal. So we built the world's first Facebook ‘AMAZE’ app to evaluate your personal Amaze score and let you compete and share it with the rest of the state.

When a user connects to Facebook through the app, we looked at a range of data like the number of tagged photos, likes, friends, events, check-ins, even the frequency of certain key words. This data was then fed into our algorithm to create a personal Amaze score.

I was part of the concepting team, I created the Interaction Design, and I created the front-end for this

www.youramaze.com.au

ECU Hits

ECU needed a more meaningful experience on their audience. So Longtail created ECU Hits, an underground radio station powered by its users. Any student, future or current, could discover, listen, and share their ultimate playlist themed around an ECU study area.

Was part of the concepting, created the interaction design, and the front-end.

www.ecuhits.com.au

Kingspark Naturescape

Rio Tinto Naturescape Kings Park is a public park in the middle of Perth, Australia. This part of the park is dedicate for children to be educated about the environment. The major challenge of this project was to create a accessible overview of the park while still maintaining an exciting user experience.
